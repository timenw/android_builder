Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how 300 Tang Poems ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Favorites and Notes: When you favorite a poem or add personal notes, this data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to any server.
  • In-App Purchases: When you purchase the Premium upgrade, the transaction is processed by Google Play. We only receive confirmation of your purchase status, not your payment details.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: We do not collect analytics, usage statistics, or crash reports.
  • Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or any other device information.

2. How We Use Information

Since we collect minimal data, our use is limited to:

  • Providing the core app functionality (poem browsing, search, favorites)
  • Processing and verifying in-app purchases through Google Play Billing
  • Displaying your locally stored favorites and notes

3. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: All your favorites, notes, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using Android's Room database and SharedPreferences.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not sync your data to any cloud service. Your data stays on your device.
  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Billing: In-app purchases are handled by Google Play. Google's privacy policy applies to transaction data: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • No Ads: The app does not use any advertising SDKs or ad networks.
  • No Analytics: The app does not use any analytics or tracking services (no Firebase, no Google Analytics, no third-party trackers).

5. Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, this policy applies equally to all users.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date
  • Using a screen lock (PIN, pattern, fingerprint, or face unlock)
  • Not rooting or jailbreaking your device

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Under GDPR, CCPA, and similar privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete your data at any time by clearing the app's data in Android Settings or uninstalling the app.
  • Portability: Premium users can export their collection as a text file.
  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell, rent, or share any user data with anyone.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Website: https://poem300.app/privacy
