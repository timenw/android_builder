## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Immerse yourself in the golden age of Chinese poetry.

300 Tang Poems brings you the most beloved poems from China's Tang Dynasty (618-907 AD) — a collection that has shaped literature for over a thousand years. Each poem features the original Chinese text, pinyin romanization, and a carefully crafted English translation with warm, accessible annotations.

Whether you're a poetry lover, a student of Chinese culture, or simply looking for a moment of beauty in your day, this app is your gateway to one of humanity's greatest literary traditions.

FEATURES:

📖 300 Complete Poems
The full collection spanning the entire Tang Dynasty, from early masters like Wang Bo to late masters like Li Shangyin and Du Mu. Includes works by Li Bai, Du Fu, Wang Wei, Bai Juyi, and 70+ other poets.

🌙 Daily Poem
Start each day with a new poem, hand-picked for the season. Let ancient wisdom set the tone for your morning.

📝 Rich Annotations
Every poem includes a warm, story-like annotation that brings the context to life — not dry academic analysis, but the kind of insight a knowledgeable friend would share.

🔍 Powerful Search
Find any poem by title, author, or even a line you half-remember. Search in Chinese or English.

📂 Browse by Theme
Explore poems by category: Nature, Love, Farewell, War, Moon, Seasons, Philosophy, and more.

❤️ Favorites & Notes
Save your favorite poems and add personal notes. Create your own poetry collection.

🎴 Shareable Quote Cards
Generate beautiful poetry cards in three styles (Minimal, Classic, Warm) and share them on social media.

🔊 Audio Readings
Listen to poems read aloud in Chinese — experience the music of the original language.

🌓 Immersive Reading Mode
Full-screen, distraction-free reading with elegant typography. Toggle translations, annotations, and your personal notes.
